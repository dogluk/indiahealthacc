# Reading

References, further reading, and people working on the India clinical validation thesis.

---

### On the clinical validation bottleneck

- Ruxandra Teslo, [*AI Won't Automatically Accelerate Clinical Trials*](https://www.asimov.press/p/ai-clinical-trials) — Asimov Press, 2026. The clearest articulation of why better drug candidates alone won't speed up clinical development. The bottleneck is operational and regulatory, not intellectual.
- Ruxandra Teslo, [*The Case for Clinical Trial Abundance*](https://ifp.org/the-case-for-clinical-trial-abundance/) — Institute for Progress, 2024. The foundational piece on why trials need to become dramatically cheaper and faster.
- Ruxandra Teslo & Jack Scannell, [*To Get More Effective Drugs, We Need More Human Trials*](https://www.macroscience.org/p/to-get-more-effective-drugs-we-need) — Macro Science, 2025.
- Saloni Dattani, Ruxandra Teslo, Adam Kroetsch et al., [*The Clinical Trials Abundance Blog*](https://www.clinicaltrialsabundance.blog/) — ongoing. Ideas and commentary on making clinical trials more efficient.
- Adam Kroetsch, [*To fix trials, we need to pay attention to the boring stuff*](https://www.clinicaltrialsabundance.blog/) — Clinical Trials Abundance Blog, 2026. Trial operations are getting less productive and more expensive.
- Ruxandra Teslo, [*Learning from Australia: towards a "Clinic-in-the-loop" future*](https://www.clinicaltrialsabundance.blog/) — Clinical Trials Abundance Blog, 2026. The Australian framework for early-stage trials shows speed and safety don't have to come at each other's expense.

### On India as an R&D base

- Owl Posting, [*How do you make a 250x better vaccine at 1/10 the cost? Develop it in India.*](https://www.owlposting.com/p/how-do-you-make-a-250x-better-vaccine) — 2025. Two-hour interview with Soham Sankaran of PopVax on why India has all the ingredients for original biotech research but almost none of it happens, and what it takes to change that.
- [*India is the pharmacy of the world. But we are losing the drug development race*](https://indianexpress.com/article/opinion/columns/india-is-pharmacy-of-world-but-losing-drug-development-race-10610013/) — Indian Express, March 2026. On the paradox of India's dominance in generics alongside its near-absence in new drug development, and the regulatory bottleneck holding it back.
- Akash Kulgod, [*The first 10 trillion dollar "company" will be an Indian Healthcare Alliance*](https://dogl.uk/10t/) — 2025.

### People and orgs

- [PopVax](https://popvax.com/) — mRNA vaccine R&D, Hyderabad. India's most compelling example of original biotech research at a fraction of US cost.
- [Dognosis](https://dognosis.tech/) — canine-AI cancer detection from breath, Bangalore.
- [1Day Sooner](https://www.1daysooner.org/) — clinical trial reform advocacy.
- [Renaissance Philanthropy](https://www.renaissancephilanthropy.org/) — clinical trial abundance fellowships. Soham Sankaran is also a fellow here.
